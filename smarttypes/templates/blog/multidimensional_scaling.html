Multidimensional Scaling (MDS)

Book on scribd

Given a set of (dis)similarities of actors (a similarity matrix), find a "good" representation as points in some lower-dimensional space (two-dimensional Euclidean space for our purposes).

Put simply, we're interested in reducing our similarity matrix to 2 dimensions.

I won't get into creating our similarity matrix. For more on that this, see Hanneman and Riddle's: Measures of similarity and structural equivalence

The essence of MDS is what you'd expect. An objective function that compares the distances in our similarity matrix w/ the distances between nodes in our proposed 2d space.
