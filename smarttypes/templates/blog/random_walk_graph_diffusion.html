Cosma Shalizi's class on Diffusion Maps

First a little praise of Cosma Shalizi.
This is the sort of guy you want teaching your kids math. He's an artist -- a poet even.
I've never heard a mathematical method referred to as 'Biblical'.
The following is from Shalizi's 2009 CMU class on Diffusion Maps

This is saying that the substance will move away from regions of high concentration and towards ones of low concentration, until it's evenly distributed everywhere. On the one hand, it's almost Biblical; on the other hand, it's the mathematical expression of what we mean when in ordinary language we say it 'oozes'.
