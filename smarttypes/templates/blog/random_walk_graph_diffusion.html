A quick overview

Random walk is a Markov chain describing the sequence of nodes visited by a random walker. This process can be described by the transition probability matrix, P, with Pxy = axy/kx presenting the probability that a random walker staying at node x will walk to y in the next step, where axy equals 1 if node x and node y are connected, 0 otherwise, and kx denotes the degree of node x.
