Biological networks

Biological networks are messy and complicated. Here's a neat quote from Péter Csermely:

"Biological networks offer the experience of a billion years in crisis-survival - we can learn from them."

This is from Péter's site:

"As an ordinary biochemist, I have started to recognize the strength of the network approach only in 2002... Currently I am working on the role of weak links in the interaction scenarios (dynamic equilibria, games) between network partners."
